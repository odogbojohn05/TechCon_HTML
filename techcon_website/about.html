Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts and has since grown into a global conference attracting thousands of attendees each year. From humble beginnings in a university auditorium to sold-out convention centers, TechCon has consistently showcased the latest innovations and fostered meaningful connections in the tech world.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the global tech community by providing a platform for learning, collaboration, and inspiration. We aim to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and real-world applications, while promoting diversity, sustainability, and ethical innovation.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Aisha Bello

Dr. Bello is a renowned AI researcher and advocate for inclusive technology. At TechCon 2022, she delivered a keynote on ethical machine learning and its impact on global development.

Mark Chen

Mark is a cybersecurity expert and founder of SecureNet. His 2021 talk on digital resilience in the age of quantum computing was a highlight of the conference.

Elena García

Elena is a robotics engineer and entrepreneur. Her presentation on human-robot collaboration in healthcare inspired many to explore robotics for social good.
